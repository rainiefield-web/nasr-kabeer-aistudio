[Source](https://www.htfmarketintelligence.com/report/global-low-carbon-aluminum-production-market)
- Global alumina and bauxite demand is projected to reach record highs by 2026, exceeding 375 million tons, largely propelled by infrastructure projects and accelerated electrification in the transportation sector, particularly EV manufacturing. [Source](https://farmonaut.com/mining/alumina-and-bauxite-market-2026-key-global-trends-outlook/)
- Primary aluminum supply remains highly concentrated, with China dominating global smelter output at approximately 59.7% of the world total (43.0 Mt in 2024e), influencing global prices and decarbonization efforts. [Source](https://statranker.org/economy/industry-and-manufacturing/top-10-aluminum-producing-countries-in-2025-global-production-insights/)
- World aluminum demand is expected to see healthy growth and price rebound, driven by increased usage in downstream industries such as aerospace, construction, and automotive. [Source](https://mcgroup.co.uk/researches/aluminium)
